Ending Recidivism and Cycles of Mass Incarceration in America

An exploration into the American justice and prison systems. This article discusses the revolving door of incarceration that plagues the US and the harm it causes to not only the inprisoned individuals, but their families and society as a whole. Current practices are analyzed and potential solutions to aid the crisis are proposed and explored.
