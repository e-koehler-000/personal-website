“Stop The Steal”: Hands Off Protestors Rally Against Trump in D.C.

Thousands of protestors gathered around the Washington Monument on Saturday April 5 as part of the Hands Off Movement’s “National Day of Action”. The rally was one of 1,300 that occurred across all 50 U.S. states on this day. The goal of the movement is to raise awareness about, and invoke action to stop, President Donald Trump’s perceived abuse of power.

Ending Recidivism and Cycles of Mass Incarceration in America

An exploration into the American justice and prison systems. This article discusses the revolving door of incarceration that plagues the US and the harm it causes to not only the inprisoned individuals, but their families and society as a whole. Current practices are analyzed and potential solutions to aid the crisis are proposed and explored.
